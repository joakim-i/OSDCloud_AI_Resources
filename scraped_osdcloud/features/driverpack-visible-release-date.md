## Feature

In the screenshot below, the OEM DriverPacks that are available are displayed with their release date so you can determine if a DriverPack is too old to apply. While these are the latest available DriverPacks from the OEM, an old DriverPack may contain vulnerabilities. In this case, you may want to not apply the DriverPack and instead use other methods, like Windows Update.
